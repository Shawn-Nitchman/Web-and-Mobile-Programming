The Halo Series

The premise of Halo

In the year 2525, human worlds come under attack by a theocratic alliance of alien races known as the Covenant.
The Covenant's superior technology and numbers prove decisive advantages; though effective, the Spartans are too
few in number to turn the tide of battle in humanity's favor. Master Chief John 117 is one of the few remaining Spartans left.

Why is Halo Popular

Halo was one of the first First Person Shooters(FPS) to come to consoles, not only that but it was able to show that a control could be used to play such a fast paced game.
Halo is also well known for it world building and interesting story along with it balanced gameplay made it a game well designed.
It was also able to bring people together by having local coop and multiplayer.
While it has come into some small trouble when the franchise switched develops it still remains many peoples favorite series after all these years.
